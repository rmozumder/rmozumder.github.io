GL4HS Research & Analysis

Brief Summary

I assembled a team of 4 students, from all over the country to work together to analyze a bioinformatic dataset. We collectively chose to analyze the metadata of OSD-771, which was collected from kidney tissue of female Mus Musculus mice (C57BL/6). More detail on the specific steps of analysis can be found in the building log. We eventually proposed a reasonable hypothesis given our data regarding spaceflight and immune conditions. The following research proposal is built on this hypothesis.
